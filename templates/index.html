Kawi Computer Literacy Program

Our Projects Based Learning approach and a tested progression-proof curriculum will give you a fun-filled headstart into computing. This program will equip you with the required computing and digital skills to remain relevant in the job market, and technologically advancing world. The program delivers computing packages through in-class discovery sessions and practicals. It delves into the fundamentals of computing (current and emerging technology); and is ideal for beginners.

*Currently available for Partner Schools only. Register your school today.

The Innovation Lab

Impactful innovations address the community's unmet needs, opportunities and challenges. In South Sudan, 70% of the population is youthful. We believe that to effectively address the problems in our communities, the young must be equipped with adequate technology skills to build solutions that matter. The Innovation Lab is a program for aspiring innovators with a focus on engineering solutions. Learners go through a foundational period of learning design thinking principles and programming in select languages. They can later choose to specialize in Development or Experience Design.

The Technology

Each student has access to a workstation for that hands-on experience. Our labs are equipped with industry standard technology.

Progression Proof Learning

Our curricula are developed, reviewed and refined by I.T. professionals and educators. Whether the learners choose to pursue a career in tech or use acquired skills for personal/professional development, our curriculum guarantees that you will be able to have the required skills for further growth.

Professional Tutors

Kawi Labs Tutors are certified IT professionals with dynamic, and indepth experience working with learners at different levels of their learning journeys.
